Lemon Poppyseed Baked Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • 1/2 spotty banana
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 2 tbsp milk of choice
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • pinch sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp poppyseeds

For the lemon icing

  • 1 tbsp powdered sugar (or sugar-free replacement)
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 and lightly oil an oven-safe baking dish.
  2. Add all ingredients to your blender and blend until fully combined.
  3. Pour batter into baking dish and bake 20-25 minutes. Let cool completely.
  4. Mix together icing ingredients and spread over baked oatmeal. Enjoy!
